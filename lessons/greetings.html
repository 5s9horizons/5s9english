Basic Greetings

Lesson 1 - A0 Level

Learning Objectives

Master basic English greetings for everyday situations
Learn when to use different greetings throughout the day

Essential Greetings

Formal Greetings

"Good morning" Before 12:00 PM

Used in professional settings and with people you don't know well

"Good afternoon" 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Common in business and formal situations

Casual Greetings

"Hi"

Friendly greeting for any time of day

"Hey"

Very casual, best for friends and family

Interactive Practice

Repetition Exercise (Call and Response)

Read the following greetings out loud and repeat them:

"Good morning"
"Good afternoon"
"Hi"
"Hey"

Matching Game

Match the greeting to the correct time of day:

What do you say at 7 AM?

Good evening
Good morning

What do you say at 2 PM?

Good afternoon
Good night

Role-Play (Basic Conversation)

Practice the following conversation:

Person A: Hello!
Person B: Hello!
Person A: Good morning!
Person B: Good morning!

Mini Quiz

Choose the correct answer: “What do you say at 7 AM?”

Good evening
Good morning

Fill in the blank: “___, my name is John.”

Hello
Good night

Fun Game

Greeting Bingo

Print out a bingo card with different greetings. Mark off each greeting as you hear it. First to get Bingo wins!
